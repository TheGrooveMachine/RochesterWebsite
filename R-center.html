R-centers

Rochester offers widespread community centers containing pools, gyms, game rooms, and excellent staff. As a member for the R-center staff myself, I am confident in saying how beneficial offering numerous opportunities to young children can be. As from swimming lessons, sports camps, and academic assistance, the R-centers pride themselves on building friendships and connections with families within the community. This overall supports the connectedness many residents feel in the city.
