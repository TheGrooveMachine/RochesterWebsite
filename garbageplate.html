The Garbage Plate

Macaroni Salad, a cheeseburger patty, a hotdog, home fries, meat sauce, onions, and bread.

First synthesized in 1918 by Nick Tahou, the garbage plate has made a name for itself in Rochester’s food community. Founder Alex Tahou named the eatery after his son, Nick, who ran the establishment until his death in 1997. Although the original Garbage Plate remains solely in the hands of the Nick Tahou brand, many copy-cat versions appeared some of which even surpassing their predecessor.
