Boss Sauce

Sweet, spicy, savory. One of the many things that make Rochester stand out. Boss Sauce, not to be confused with country sweet, is a locally c reated sauce often found on wings, subs, and cheesesteaks.

American Specialty Manufacturing Company Inc. was formed in 1983, in Rochester, New York. We are a privately held company that exclusively manufactures “Boss Sauce.” The company opened its first location on Canal Street. Once more people tasted that one-of-a-kind savory after sauce, the popularity of Boss Sauce grew. After several years of tremendous growth, we moved to a large facility at 272 Hudson Avenue, Rochester, New York, 14605. We are currently at this location today and celebrating nearly 40 years of business, health, and growth.
