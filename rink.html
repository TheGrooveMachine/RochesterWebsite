Ice Rinks

In addition to the R-centers, the city of Rochester also provides 2 public ice rinks to its residents. One is located on 131 Elmwood avenue and the other in Downtown Rochester in Martin Luther King Jr. Square. Aside from offering normal regular free skate hours, the rinks host hockey practices, games, as well as holiday-themed events. These include a haunted house as well as a holiday village in december, which attracts food trucks and people from all over the city.
