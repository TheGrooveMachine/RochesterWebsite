Colleges in Rochester

Unlike its city schools, Rochester offers some of the most advanced and renowned universities in the nation. The University of Rochester and RIT rank among the top schools in the United States and regular attract students not only nationally, but internationally as well. To quote RIT’s president David Munson Jr., “RIT… is today one of the top universities in the nation working at the intersection of technology, the arts and design. We are a university that is shaping the future and improving the world through creativity and innovation.” Furthermore, in terms of diversity, both colleges offer impressive statistics showing that people from many different deomgraphics attend the schools.
